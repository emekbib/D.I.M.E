About Us

Diversity and Inclusion for Minority Engineers

Welcome to D.I.M.E (Diversity and Inclusion for Minority Engineers)! We're passionate about leveling the playing field in engineering by fostering a more inclusive industry. Our mission is to empower aspiring engineers while creating opportunities for underrepresented talent.

At D.I.M.E, we're dedicated to our vision and goals, outlined in our explanation document, focusing on strategies that shape our organization's future. We invite you to review this and join us at our upcoming June meeting to discuss and contribute your insights, which are invaluable in steering our direction.

During our sessions, we delve into key initiatives: mentorship programs, networking opportunities, advocacy efforts, and steps bridging the gap for minority engineering students. We believe in making a significant impact by providing guidance, fostering connections, and advocating for diversity and inclusion in the engineering sector.

Micheal Abebe CEO-Founder
